Free Classes

We offer free classes to all members of the public on basic computer maintenance and upkeep as needed. Fill out the form below and sign up for one of our classes and we'll send you an email with dates we've scheduled. These classes are TOTALLY FREE and come with no catches or obligations. You may be asking why we're willing to teach people things that will make our jobs just a little bit less secure. The answer is because we'd rather you not experience the type of situation where you need to call us; that means something has gone really rather wrong, and we'd like to stop that from happening.
