With his extensive background in Service Industry jobs, he knew how to treat a customer, especially if you wanted them to bring their business back, and he incorporated that into his philosophy. neRDS Inc. was officially founded in late 2019 and has since exploded into popularity across the entire Roanoke and New River Valley area.
